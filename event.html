Grace Charis Pro Am Championship Event Details

Schedule

  • 21 March 2026, Registration & warm-up from 7:00 AM; tee times begin at 8:00 AM

Featured Guests & Stars

The A Golfer’s Dream Charity Event will bring together legendary golfers such as Tiger Woods, Jack Nicklaus, Gary Player, and Lee Trevino, joined by other prominent stars from the worlds of sports, entertainment, and philanthropy, all united in support of less privileged and sick children across the United States.

Line-up is subject to change. Appearance confirmations and final schedule will be shared closer to the event.
